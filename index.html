Who

Kid Cudi is an American rapper best known for his revolutionary approach to hip-hop which has earned him the respect,

friendship and mentorship of big names in rap. He first caught the attention of Kanye West with his debut mixtape and

arrived in the hip-hop world in grand style with hit single “Day N Nite.”

He has continued to inspire and innovate ever since.
